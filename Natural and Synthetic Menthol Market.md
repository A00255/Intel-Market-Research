Menthol is a naturally occurring organic compound with the chemical formula C10H20O, commonly extracted from mint leaves or synthesized in laboratories. It exists as a crystalline solid and is widely used for its cooling and soothing effects. Menthol is an essential ingredient in pharmaceuticals, oral hygiene products, confectioneries, and tobacco products. The global Natural Menthol and Synthetic Menthol market consists of both natural and synthetic menthol production and distribution across various industries.
