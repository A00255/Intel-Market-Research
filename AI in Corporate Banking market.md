AI in Corporate Banking refers to the integration of artificial intelligence technologies—such as machine learning (ML), natural language processing (NLP), and robotic process automation (RPA)—into corporate banking services. These technologies enable banks to automate processes, improve customer experiences, detect fraud, and enhance decision-making capabilities in areas such as credit scoring, loan underwriting, trade finance, regulatory compliance, and more. The use of AI transforms traditional corporate banking by optimizing operations, reducing costs, and delivering personalized services at scale.
