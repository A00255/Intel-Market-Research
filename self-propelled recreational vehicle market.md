Self-propelled recreational vehicles (RVs) are motorized vehicles designed for travel, leisure, and camping activities. Unlike towable RVs, these vehicles integrate both driving and living facilities, offering enhanced mobility and convenience. They are widely used for personal travel, tourism, and adventure purposes, catering to individual owners, rental services, and government entities.
