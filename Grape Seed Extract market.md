Organic grape seed extract (OGSE) is a natural supplement derived from the seeds of organically cultivated grapes. Rich in proanthocyanidins—potent antioxidants—OGSE is renowned for its health benefits, including cardiovascular support, anti-inflammatory properties, and skin health enhancement. Its extraction involves environmentally friendly methods, ensuring the preservation of its bioactive compounds. OGSE is widely utilized across various industries, such as nutraceuticals, cosmetics, and functional foods, owing to its therapeutic properties and consumer demand for natural products.
