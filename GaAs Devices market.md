Gallium Arsenide (GaAs) Devices are specialized radio frequency (RF) components fabricated using the compound semiconductor gallium arsenide. These devices outperform traditional silicon-based semiconductors in high-frequency applications due to GaAs's exceptional electron mobility, low noise generation, and high-power handling capabilities. GaAs devices are pivotal in high-speed, high-frequency, and precision-dependent applications such as wireless communication, satellite technology, radar systems, and optoelectronics. With growing demand across multiple industries including mobile communication, automotive, and defense, GaAs technology is increasingly seen as indispensable in modern RF systems.
