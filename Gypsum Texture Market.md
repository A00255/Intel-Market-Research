The Gypsum Texture Market encompasses a wide range of drywall textures, also known as gypsum board textures, which are used to enhance the aesthetic appeal of interior walls while concealing drywall seams and imperfections. These textures play a crucial role in interior decoration by adding depth and reflectivity to walls. The market covers both drywall texture products and repair products essential for maintaining wall finishes.
