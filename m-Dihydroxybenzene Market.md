m-Dihydroxybenzene, also known as hydroquinone or benzene-1,4-diol, is a colorless crystalline organic compound with the formula C6H4(OH)2. It is soluble in water, alcohol, and organic solvents and is widely used across various industries such as pharmaceuticals, agrochemicals, textiles, paper, and resin production.
