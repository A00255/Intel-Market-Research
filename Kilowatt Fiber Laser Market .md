Kilowatt fiber lasers are high-powered lasers that use optical fibers as their gain medium. These lasers provide superior beam quality, efficiency, and reliability compared to traditional solid-state or CO2 lasers. Their applications span various industries, including manufacturing, medical, and telecommunications, where they play a critical role in material processing, cutting, welding, and marking.
