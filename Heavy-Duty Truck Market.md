Heavy-duty trucks and tractors are the most robust and largest types of vehicles legally permitted on roadways. These vehicles are specially engineered for long-haul transportation and are pivotal in industrial and commercial logistics. Primarily used to transport large quantities of goods across vast distances, heavy-duty trucks include complete vehicles, incomplete vehicles, and semitrailer tractors. Their exceptional load-bearing capacity and endurance make them indispensable in sectors like infrastructure development, construction, and freight logistics. As a vital part of the global logistics ecosystem, heavy-duty trucks significantly contribute to economic development by ensuring a steady flow of goods and materials.
