Space DC-DC Converters are specialized power conversion devices designed for space applications, facilitating the conversion of direct current (DC) from one voltage level to another. These converters must meet stringent requirements to withstand the extreme conditions of space, including high radiation exposure, extreme temperatures, and the need for high reliability. They play a crucial role in powering satellites, spacecraft, and various space-related systems.
