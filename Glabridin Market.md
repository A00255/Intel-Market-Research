Glabridin is a natural compound extracted from licorice root, widely recognized for its potent skin-lightening and anti-inflammatory properties. It is extensively used in the cosmetic and pharmaceutical industries, primarily in skin care formulations such as creams, lotions, sunscreens, and face masks. Due to its strong antioxidant and melanin-inhibiting capabilities, Glabridin is gaining traction as a critical ingredient in skin-brightening and anti-aging products.
