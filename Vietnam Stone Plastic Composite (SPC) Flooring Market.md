Stone Plastic Composite (SPC) flooring is an innovative rigid core flooring solution composed of limestone powder, polyvinyl chloride (PVC), and stabilizers. This unique composition provides exceptional durability, water resistance, and dimensional stability, making SPC flooring a preferred choice for both residential and commercial applications. It is highly resistant to impact, moisture, and temperature fluctuations, ensuring long-lasting performance and easy maintenance.
