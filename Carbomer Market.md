
The global Carbomer market was valued at US$ 667.74 million in 2023 and is anticipated to reach US$ 938.1 million by 2030, witnessing a CAGR of 5.75% during the forecast period 2024-2030.

Carbomer is a generic term for a group of synthetic polymers that are used as rheology modifiers, gelling agents, and thickeners in various personal care products, such as hair gels, creams, lotions, and eye drops.
