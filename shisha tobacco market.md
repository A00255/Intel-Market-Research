Shisha tobacco, also known as hookah tobacco, is a flavored tobacco product that is smoked through a water pipe or hookah. It is often infused with various flavors such as fruit, mint, and other exotic blends. The market for shisha tobacco is driven by the increasing social acceptance of hookah smoking in bars, cafes, and lounges, as well as the growing popularity of premium tobacco products.
