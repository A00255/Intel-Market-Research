
C5 petrochemicals refer to hydrocarbons derived from the fifth carbon fraction of petroleum refining. These chemicals include isoprene, pentanes, and cyclopentadiene and are widely used in applications such as synthetic rubber, adhesives, resins, and chemical intermediates. C5 petrochemicals are essential in manufacturing products like tires, plastics, and coatings.
