
The Duty-Free and Travel Retail Market  comprises retail outlets that are exempt from certain local or national taxes and duties, provided that the goods sold are intended for travelers exiting the country. This market operates primarily in international airports, border crossings, seaports, and onboard airlines, offering a range of products such as luxury goods, tobacco, alcohol, personal care items, and more. The scope of duty-free products varies by jurisdiction, affecting the sales and business operations within the sector.
