B2B mold inhibitors are specialized chemical agents used to prevent mold growth in various industrial applications. These inhibitors are widely employed in industries such as plastics, textiles, building materials, and housing equipment to enhance product longevity and safety. They work by disrupting mold reproduction and growth, ensuring the quality and durability of materials.
