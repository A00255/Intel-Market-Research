A PIN Photo Diode is a specialized semiconductor device used for light detection and conversion into an electrical current. The device consists of three layers: a p-type semiconductor layer, an intrinsic semiconductor layer, and an n-type semiconductor layer. This unique structure enables high-speed response and sensitivity, making PIN photo diodes widely used in various applications such as optical communication, LiDAR, medical imaging, and consumer electronics.
