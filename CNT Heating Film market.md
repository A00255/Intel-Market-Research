CNT (Carbon Nanotube) Heating Film is an advanced heating technology utilizing carbon nanotubes to generate efficient, uniform, and flexible heat distribution. These films are widely applied in industries such as construction, automotive, aerospace, and medical equipment due to their energy efficiency, lightweight nature, and adaptability.
