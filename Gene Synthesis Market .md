The Gene Synthesis Market refers to the global industry focused on the artificial creation of DNA sequences or genes in laboratories, using chemical synthesis. This technology enables researchers and companies to design and construct genetic materials without the need for a pre-existing DNA template. Gene synthesis is an essential tool in synthetic biology, biotechnology, and genomics, facilitating advances in areas such as drug development, agriculture, and genetic engineering. Key products include synthetic DNA, oligonucleotides, and custom genes, which are used across various industries for research, therapeutics, and industrial applications.
