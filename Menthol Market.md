
Menthol is a naturally occurring organic compound derived from peppermint and other mint oils. It is widely used in various industries, including pharmaceuticals, personal care, food and beverages, and tobacco, due to its cooling sensation and medicinal properties. The menthol market is divided into synthetic and natural menthol, catering to multiple applications such as oral hygiene, medicine, confectionery, and more.
