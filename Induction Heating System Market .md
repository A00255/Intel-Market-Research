

Induction Heating Systems refer to advanced thermal processing solutions that utilize electromagnetic induction to heat electrically conductive materials. These systems generate heat through eddy currents induced in the material, providing a precise and efficient heating method. They are widely used in industrial applications such as metal hardening, welding, and forging, as well as in the semiconductor industry and other niche applications. Key components include induction coils, power sources, and cooling systems.
