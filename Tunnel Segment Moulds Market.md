
Tunnel segment moulds are specialized manufacturing forms utilized to cast precast concrete segments for tunnel construction. These moulds are designed to produce highly accurate, consistent, and durable tunnel segments, which are essential for constructing safe and robust tunnel linings. The precision engineering of these moulds ensures uniformity in shape, size, and strength, meeting the stringent requirements of modern infrastructure projects. Tunnel segments, produced using these moulds, are pivotal components in subway tunnels, railway and road tunnels, water infrastructure, and other subterranean constructions.
