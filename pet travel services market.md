The global pet travel services market encompasses businesses and services that facilitate the safe and convenient transportation of pets domestically and internationally. These services include pet-friendly airlines, pet relocation services, specialized pet transport, and pet-friendly accommodations. With the growing trend of pet humanization, more pet owners are seeking reliable and professional travel solutions to ensure their pets’ comfort and safety during journeys.
