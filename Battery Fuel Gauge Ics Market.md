Battery Fuel Gauge ICs are integrated circuits designed to monitor and report the state of charge (SoC) and state of health (SoH) of batteries in electronic devices. These ICs help optimize battery performance, enhance energy efficiency, and ensure safe operation in various applications, including mobile devices, medical equipment, and robotics.
