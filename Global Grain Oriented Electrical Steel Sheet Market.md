
Grain Oriented Electrical Steel Sheet (GOES) is a specialized steel product designed for high-efficiency energy transmission and electromagnetic applications. It is primarily used in transformer cores, electric motors, and generators, where its unique grain structure minimizes energy loss and enhances magnetic properties. This steel type is processed through precise rolling and annealing techniques to align its grain structure, optimizing its performance in electromagnetic applications.
