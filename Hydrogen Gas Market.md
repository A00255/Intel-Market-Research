Hydrogen Gas Market refers to the global trade and utilization of hydrogen (H2) gas, a colorless, odorless, and tasteless element. Known for being the lightest element, hydrogen is extensively produced via natural gas steam reforming and the electrolysis of water. It is widely recognized as the cleanest fuel, as its combustion yields only water vapor and no carbon emissions. The market encompasses both captive hydrogen (produced and consumed onsite) and merchant hydrogen (produced centrally and distributed to end-users).
