The global Prasterone (CAS 53-43-0) market was valued at US$ 49.28 million in 2024 and is projected to reach US$ 75.92 million by 2031, growing at a CAGR of 7.17% during the forecast period (2025-2031).

Prasterone (CAS 53-43-0), commonly known as dehydroepiandrosterone (DHEA), is a naturally occurring steroid hormone primarily produced in the adrenal glands. It serves as a precursor to essential hormones like estrogen and testosterone. Prasterone has applications in pharmaceuticals, dietary supplements, skincare products, and laboratory reagents. It is widely recognized for its role in aging management, hormone therapy, and immune system enhancement.
