
Plastic pipe fittings are components used to connect, control, or terminate plastic piping systems. These fittings are primarily manufactured using materials such as PVC, CPVC, ABS, PE, and Nylon. They are widely utilized across various industries, including residential, commercial, industrial, and agricultural applications. Their advantages over traditional metal fittings include corrosion resistance, lightweight properties, cost-effectiveness, and ease of installation.
