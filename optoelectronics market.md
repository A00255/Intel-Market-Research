Optoelectronics refers to the study and application of electronic devices that source, detect, and control light. These devices convert electrical signals into optical signals and vice versa, playing a crucial role in industries such as aerospace, defense, consumer electronics, automotive, medical, and industrial applications. Key optoelectronic components include photodiodes, photomultiplier tubes, optocouplers, light-emitting diodes (LEDs), laser diodes, and optoelectronic oscillators.
