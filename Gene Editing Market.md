Gene Editing Market refers to the sector focused on technologies that enable the precise alteration of genetic material within organisms. These technologies are utilized in various domains including biomedical research, agriculture, and therapeutic development. Key gene editing tools include CRISPR-Cas9, TALENs (Transcription Activator-Like Effector Nucleases), and ZFNs (Zinc Finger Nucleases), each offering unique methods to introduce modifications into the DNA sequence of an organism. Gene editing plays a critical role in advancing personalized medicine, curing genetic disorders, and engineering crops with enhanced traits.
