On-Demand Manufacturing Service refers to a production model where manufacturing is executed as per customer demand rather than mass production. This model leverages advanced digital manufacturing technologies such as 3D printing, CNC machining, and injection molding, allowing companies to produce customized parts and products efficiently. It reduces inventory costs, minimizes waste, and speeds up product development cycles, making it a preferred choice in industries such as automotive, aerospace, healthcare, and electronics.
