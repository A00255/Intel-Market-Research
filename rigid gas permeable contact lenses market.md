Rigid gas permeable (RGP) contact lenses are a type of hard contact lens that allow oxygen to pass through to the cornea, offering superior vision correction and durability compared to soft lenses. Unlike traditional hard lenses, RGP lenses provide enhanced breathability, reducing the risk of eye infections and improving overall eye health.
