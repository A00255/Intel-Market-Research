
Polyethylene Terephthalate Glycol (PTEG) Pellets are thermoplastic polyester materials known for their clarity, durability, and chemical resistance. These pellets are widely used in various industries such as automotive, food packaging, and 3D printing due to their excellent mechanical properties and recyclability.
