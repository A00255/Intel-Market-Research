Glass screen protectors vary in thickness, ranging from 0.15 mm to 0.4 mm, with 0.33 mm being the most popular due to its balance of strength and usability. As consumer dependency on smartphones and tablets rises, the demand for durable, reliable, and aesthetically seamless screen protectors continues to grow.
