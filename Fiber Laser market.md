Fiber lasers are a type of laser that uses an optical fiber as the active gain medium, typically doped with rare-earth elements such as erbium, ytterbium, or neodymium. These lasers are known for their high efficiency, robust design, and versatility, making them a preferred choice in industries such as manufacturing, telecommunications, and medical applications. Fiber lasers can emit high-powered, coherent light beams with exceptional beam quality, which is ideal for precision tasks such as cutting, welding, marking, and engraving.
