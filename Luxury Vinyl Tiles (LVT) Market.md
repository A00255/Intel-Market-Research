Luxury Vinyl Tiles (LVT) are premium, resilient floor coverings made from high-quality polyvinyl chloride (PVC). LVT is designed to mimic the aesthetics of natural materials like wood, stone, and ceramic while providing enhanced durability and ease of maintenance. These tiles are often preferred in both residential and commercial settings due to their cost-effectiveness, water resistance, and design versatility.
